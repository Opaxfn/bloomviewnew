Top 7 Twitter Prospecting Tools in 2023

‍

WHAT IS TWITTER PROSPECTING?

Twitter can be highly effective for lead prospecting. By using this platform, you can establish relationships with potential customers, demonstrate your company's expertise, and convert prospects into paying customers.

The primary objective of using Twitter is not to make an immediate sale with every tweet. Instead, it is about building a foundation for a relationship with prospects. By doing so, you can gain their trust and make them feel more at ease when communicating with you through email or phone calls.

‍‍

WHAT DOES A TWITTER PROSPECTING TOOL DO?

There are two different methods for approaching leads on Twitter:

Soft approach: This involves actions that generate a notification, such as liking and retweeting the lead's content.

Direct approach: This involves interacting directly with the lead, such as tweeting at them or sending them direct messages (DMs). However, it's important to note that to send a DM, the recipient must either have opted in to receive DMs from anyone or be following you.

To increase the chances of being able to send a DM, it's recommended to follow the lead for a few days beforehand, in case they follow you back and allow you to send them a DM if they haven't already done so.

BEST TWITTER PROSPECTING TOOLS IN 2023

‍

1. SOLIDINBOX

‍

Solidinbox is a useful tool that can filter out relevant Twitter profiles or leads and send them personalized direct messages (DMs) automatically, resulting in more replies and revenue. It helps save time by finding new customers on Twitter and unlocking growth through DM automation. 

Using Solidinbox, you can search for new leads and personalize DMs based on various criteria such as keywords, tweets, and verification status. The tool offers over 10 filters to help you find leads with precision. Additionally, you can export leads and obtain their email data or reach out to them directly through Solidinbox.

Pricing: SolidInbox starts at $39 per user per month.

2. LAGROWTHMACHINE

‍

LaGrowthMachine is an excellent tool that has enabled us to automate our outbound marketing efforts, including follow-ups. This has allowed us to concentrate on tasks that deliver high value.

LaGrowthMachine is a multi-channel prospecting platform that engages in conversations on LinkedIn, email, and Twitter. It is an ideal solution for growth teams, sales teams, and recruiters who want to scale their processes and achieve 3.5 times more replies.

Pricing:  LaGrowthMachine plans start at 220€ per month, per identity.

Rating: LaGrowthMachine is rated 4.8/5 on G2 (28 reviews)

3. CONTACTBIRD

‍

ContactBird offers a solution to service-based businesses that eliminates concerns about client acquisition. With ContactBird, you can automate your cold DM outreach without worrying about being banned. You can also search for users based on specific criteria such as followers of a particular user, users who follow a certain user, or users who use certain keywords and then save them to a list.

Pricing: Starting at $49 per month, ContactBird offers a plan that includes 10,000 active leads and allows for up to 50 messages to be sent per day.

4. COLDDM

‍

An initial message sent to a recipient on social networks like LinkedIn or Twitter with the aim of gaining a benefit in terms of favor, sales, or any other opportunity is known as a cold DM.

Cold DM is a community of people who either want to reach out to others of interest or want to share their knowledge and listen to new opportunities, or in most cases, both. If crafted appropriately and directed at the right people, Twitter cold DMs can be a powerful marketing channel. Cold DM assists with both.

Cold DM enables you to find high-quality leads, generate AI-powered templates, run personalized outreach campaigns, and increase your sales and brand awareness. With our advanced Twitter indexing, you can instantly discover thousands of leads best suited to your needs. You can review our response rate metrics to identify leads who are more likely to respond.

Pricing: ColdDM plans start at $20 which is perfect for solopreneurs and marketers

5. TWEET HUNTER

‍

The all-in-one Twitter growth tool called Tweet Hunter is aimed at helping users effortlessly grow and monetize their Twitter audience. The tool's objective is to simplify the process of creating high-performing content, building a dedicated audience around your areas of expertise, and drawing attention and monetization opportunities.

If you're looking to streamline your Twitter activity, increase your following, and generate viral tweets using AI, Tweet Hunter is a compelling new option to consider. Through Twitter automation, the tool helps you save time and effort by scheduling tweets for maximum impact and reach.

‍Pricing: Tweet Hunter plans start at $49/mo.

‍Ratings: Tweet Hunter is rated 1.5/5 on G1 (1 review)

6. SOCIALOOMPH

‍

SocialOomph is an indispensable tool to have in your social media marketing toolkit, offering a range of intriguing Twitter automation features. One such feature enables you to send DMs to new followers while also automatically following people who have just followed you and unfollowing those who've unfollowed your account. These functions are useful in cultivating an audience that is genuinely interested in your Twitter content.

In addition to post-scheduling automation and the ability to include RSS feeds in your tweets, this Twitter automation tool boasts the Email Reply Digest feature. This feature is particularly valuable for businesses as it sends a daily email containing all brand mentions made on Twitter.

Pricing: SocialOomph plans start at $55 per month.

Rating:  SocialOomph is rated 3.9/5 on G2 (21 reviews)

7. STATUSBREW

 

To keep your followers satisfied, it's crucial to send targeted messages at appropriate intervals. If you intend to automate your direct messages on Twitter, it's advisable to choose a tool that personalizes your communication. For this purpose, StatusBrew is an excellent Twitter automation tool to consider.

StatusBrew is a remarkable social media tool that offers several useful features, including social media automated publishing, brand tracking, customer engagement, and reporting.

Pricing: StatusBrew starts at $89 per mo

CONCLUSION

Twitter can be an invaluable tool for prospecting as it provides insight into what's currently relevant and on the minds of your prospects. By utilizing Twitter lists, you can efficiently monitor your prospects' daily activity to identify opportunities for starting conversations, whether it's online or over the phone. By offering them content that's pertinent and valuable, you can position yourself as a thought leader and remain at the forefront of their minds when they're in the process of making a purchase. As a result, when you speak with them offline, you'll be able to engage in more meaningful conversations centered around their needs and how you can provide value to them.

FAQ

How do I find prospects on Twitter?

When it comes to Twitter, it’s all about who you follow and who follows you. With more than 300 million people to choose from, it will take a bit of effort to find your target audience. Start by identifying and searching for keywords and hashtags your potential buyers might use.

How to use Twitter as a powerful social prospecting tool?

The most important asset in the world is data. Take advantage of the opportunity and start building your database.

It doesn’t matter if you are using Excel, Buzzstream, or something else, just start.

Why are Twitter prospecting tools important?

Twitter prospecting tools are important because they provide an efficient and effective way to identify and engage with potential customers or clients on Twitter. These tools can help you monitor and analyze your prospects' activity on Twitter, such as their interests, conversations, and behavior, allowing you to better understand their needs and preferences.
