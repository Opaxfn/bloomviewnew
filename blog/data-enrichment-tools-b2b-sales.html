7 Best Data Enrichment Tools to Boost Your B2B Sales in 2024

‍

How many hours have you spent scouring the web for a lead’s contact information, only for your email to bounce back? How long have you spent researching a target’s tech stack, all to find that your intel was out of date? 

Now, imagine if there was a tool that could do the same research in a fraction of the time, all while verifying every data point along the way. This is precisely what data enrichment tools were built to do. 

‍

What are data enrichment tools, and why do you need one?

‍

Go-to-market (GTM) teams use data enrichment tools to supplement existing data by scraping third-party sources like social media platforms, databases, webpages, and more. But the process doesn’t stop there: Data enrichment tools also help GTM teams by verifying new data and appending existing data to ensure that it’s as accurate as can be before they begin their outreach.  

With huge time savings and impressive insights, data enrichment tools can be transformative for GTM teams who are looking to revamp their strategies and streamline their workflows. Let’s dive a little deeper into the myriad of benefits that data enrichment tools can offer. 

‍

1. Enhanced targeting and segmentation

‍GTM teams can leverage data enrichment tools to make sure that leads fit into their ideal customer profile (ICP) before investing further time in outreach. Ultimately, over a quarter of surveyed marketers reported that having a “single source of truth,” like an enriched CRM, increased their strategy effectiveness and ROI. 

‍

2. Stronger relationships

‍Over 90% of surveyed marketers said that their customers expect a personalized experience. It’s important for SDRs to develop a rapport with prospects right off the bat—and enriched data helps them to do just that by giving them information to tailor outreach to prospects’ unique needs and preferences. 

‍

3. Better conversion rates

‍Personalized outreach not only reduces churn, but also accelerates a prospect’s journey through the marketing funnel. According to HubSpot, 94% of marketers say that personalization is important for improving sales. 

‍

4. Faster prospecting workflows

‍AI can save GTM teams up to 4 hours a day when it comes to writing and researching emails. Automated workflows can also streamline tasks like building lists and managing CRMs for additional time savings. 

‍

How do you choose the best data enrichment tool for you?

No two companies are exactly alike—which means that no two companies will have the exact same data enrichment needs. However, choosing the right tool can streamline list-building, improve targeting and segmentation, and ensure compliance during every stage of the marketing funnel. 

‍

1. Data coverage

‍Reliable data is fundamental for precise targeting and segmentation. An effective data enrichment tool continuously verifies and appends data to maintain accuracy and relevance.‍

2. Integrations

‍It’s important for data enrichment tools to connect seamlessly with your existing tech stack in order to efficiently manage your customer relationships and campaigns. 

3. Automation

‍AI-powered workflows like web scraping, data enrichment, and email drafting can reduce operational costs for SDRs and GTM leaders alike.

4. Compliance

Compliance with industry standards like GDPR, CCPA, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 is essential for safeguarding data privacy and maintaining customer trust.

FAQs

What is data enrichment, and how does it work?

B2B data enrichment involves enhancing existing datasets (like lead lists) with additional information gathered from various sources, such as social media platforms, databases, and webpages. This process not only fills in missing details, but also verifies and appends data points that may be stale or out of date. 

What’s an example of data enrichment?

Imagine that an SDR discovers a handful of leads via their LinkedIn network, but wants to investigate further to make sure that each one matches their ICP. Data enrichment tools could help by augmenting the existing data that the SDR has for each lead. This might include information like each lead’s company size, funding stage, tech stack, and more. Ultimately, this enriched data set might give the SDR a better picture of each lead’s needs, and help the SDR to allocate their time to the most promising ones.

How do data enrichment tools work with CRMs? 

Data enrichment tools integrate with CRMs by automatically updating (or appending) CRM records with fresh, verified information. This ensures that GTM teams always have up-to-date data readily available for use when building out ICPs, conducting outreach to prospects, and more.

How can GTM teams use data enrichment?

GTM teams leverage data enrichment tools in a multitude of ways, from refining their targeting to sending personalized outreach. By enriching their existing data with additional personal and company details, GTM teams can home in on prospects more quickly, and then send outreach that resonates with their specific needs and preferences. All in all, data enrichment helps GTM teams to optimize their marketing strategies and boost their conversion rates, all while saving on operational costs.
